About House Of Brilliance

At the House of Brilliance, we are changing the diamond buying experience. We are a division of an importer and exporter of wholesale diamonds and fine jewelry that has been one the most influential organizations in shaping the diamond market in the Dallas metroplex. Some of Dallas’s most recognized diamond wholesalers and retailers got their start with us or working for us. With over 35 years of experience supplying wholesalers and retailers nationwide, we are the source for diamonds and fine jewelry. At the House of Brilliance, our customers can compare our extensive selection of fine diamonds in a pressure free environment. Our diamonds represent the best value at the most competitive wholesale prices you will find with laboratory grading from the GIA, AGS, IGI, EGL USA, and HRD.



As part of our commitment to optimal value and unforgettable jewelry, our customers can choose from our ready manufactured fine jewelry, build their own ring from an elegant selection of styles, or create a custom piece. The House of Brilliance team of dedicated jewelry designers, diamond experts, and sales associates work hand in hand to achieve your highest level of satisfaction.
